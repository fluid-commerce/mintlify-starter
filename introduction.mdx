Fluid is a We-Commerce platform built for merchants and direct sales teams who want to sell together — not in silos. It unifies your storefront, sales rep attribution, global payments, and customer identity into a single connected system, so every party in a sale gets full credit and visibility. Whether you're launching a new product, onboarding a network of reps, or expanding into new markets, Fluid gives you the infrastructure to grow without stitching together dozens of third-party tools.

## Key Platform Components

Fluid is composed of several purpose-built modules that work independently or together. Here's a quick orientation to the most commonly used components.

### FairShare Attribution

FairShare is Fluid's JavaScript attribution SDK. It tracks which sales rep deserves credit for a purchase by following cookies, fingerprints, and referral routes — even across domains you don't own. Install one script tag on any site and FairShare silently attributes every conversion back to the right rep.

### Fluid Payments

Fluid Payments is a global payment aggregator that supports 300+ alternative payment methods (APMs) across 100+ countries. Accept Apple Pay, Google Pay, GCash, AliPay, local bank transfers, and dozens of regional wallets without managing separate payment processor accounts. All card data is stored in Fluid's PCI-compliant vault.

### We-Commerce Checkout

Fluid's one-page checkout lives at [checkout.fluid.app](https://checkout.fluid.app) and is shared across every Fluid-powered storefront. Returning customers check out instantly using saved profiles, reducing cart abandonment without any extra development work on your end.

### Website Editor

The Website Editor is a drag-and-drop site builder that uses Liquid templating. Start from one of three themes — Base, Vox, or Fluid — and customize your storefront without writing a line of code. Developers can drop into the template layer at any time for full control.

### Droplet Marketplace

Droplets are pre-built third-party app integrations you can add to your store with a single click. Because every Droplet is built against Fluid's native APIs, they work seamlessly with your checkout, rep attribution, and commission engine — no middleware to configure or maintain.
